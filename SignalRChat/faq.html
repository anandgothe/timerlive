Frequently Asked Questions

Q1) What is this session name?
A) It's anything you type! Your choice. It helps you connect to the same countdown from a different device/browser. Session name is case-sensitive.

Q2) Why does the timer continue to tick even after it reached zero?
A) So that you can see who long over the time you have gone!

Q3) Once the timer is ticking, how do I change it?
A) Click on the expand icon on top of the timer.

Q4) How to see the same countdown on different devices?
A) Just open your browser, visit timerlive.com, type in the same session name that you used on the other device.

Q5) How to change the timer from a different device?
A) Just open your browser, visit timerlive.com, type in the same session name that you used on other the device, then see the answer to question Q3 above.
